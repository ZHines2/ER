## Game Mechanics Glossary

### Core Resources
- **Pixels** - Primary resource generated when balls hit walls. Used for most basic upgrades and buildings.
- **Energy** - Secondary resource generated from ball collisions. Powers more advanced systems.
- **Science** - Generated from ball-to-ball collisions and buildings. Unlocks research and advanced features.
- **Quantum** - Advanced resource generated by special balls. Required for high-tier content.
- **Antimatter** - End-game resource with extremely powerful applications.

### Ball Types
- **Basic Ball** - Standard gray ball. Balanced pixel and energy generation.
- **Energy Ball** - Blue ball focused on energy generation. Slower but more efficient for energy.
- **Quantum Ball** - Orange ball with quantum generation capabilities. Fast and agile.
- **Antimatter Ball** - Red ball for end-game content. Massive resource generation potential.

### Ball Physics & Mechanics
- **Wall Collisions** - Generate pixels and energy based on ball type and upgrades
- **Ball-to-Ball Collisions** - Generate science, especially when different ball types collide
- **Velocity & Mass** - Each ball type has different physics properties affecting collisions
- **Vitality System** - Balls decay over time through collisions, reducing effectiveness
- **Decay Rate** - How quickly balls lose vitality during ball-to-ball collisions
- **Emergency Spawning** - Prevents total ball extinction with automatic emergency ball creation

### Upgrades (One-time Purchases)
- **Speed Upgrades** - Increase ball velocity for more frequent collisions
- **Resource Multipliers** - Multiply pixel/energy generation (costs increase exponentially)
- **Ball Spawning** - Add additional balls to the simulation
- **Efficiency Boosts** - Stackable percentage improvements to various systems
- **Special Ball Unlocks** - Unlock energy, quantum, and antimatter ball types
- **Automation** - Auto-clicking and auto-buying systems
- **Vitality Boosts** - Improve ball health and decay resistance

### Buildings (Passive Income)
#### Tier 1 - Basic Buildings
- **Pixel Generator** - Passive pixel production
- **Collector** - Passive energy production  
- **Lab** - Passive science production

#### Tier 1.5 - Intermediate Buildings
- **Amplifier** - Enhanced pixel and energy generation
- **Conduit** - Improved energy and science production
- **Synthesizer** - Multi-resource production
- **Resonator** - Science and pixel synergy

#### Tier 2+ - Advanced Buildings
- **Quantum Generator** - Produces quantum resources
- **Antimatter Generator** - End-game antimatter production
- **Mega Lab** - High-efficiency science production
- **Auto Factory** - Automated resource production

#### Tier 2-4 - Vitality Support Buildings
- **Vitality Station** - Reduces global decay rate
- **Rejuvenator** - Provides vitality recovery during collisions
- **Eternity Core** - Can completely eliminate decay when built

### Research (Permanent Unlocks)
#### Basic Research
- **Physics** - Increase ball velocity
- **Cache** - Improve save frequency
- **Collision Bonus** - Multiply collision rewards

#### Intermediate Research  
- **Momentum Theory** - Boost momentum-based effects
- **Wave Harmonics** - Harmonic resonance bonuses
- **Field Theory** - Advanced field manipulation
- **Entanglement** - Quantum preparation effects

#### Advanced Research
- **Quantum Theory** - Unlock quantum mechanics
- **Time Acceleration** - Speed up passive generation
- **Prestige System** - Enable meta-progression
- **Vitality Theory** - Improve maximum ball health
- **Decay Nullification** - Completely disable decay system

### Prestige System
- **Prestige Levels** - Meta-progression layers that reset most progress
- **Prestige Points** - Currency earned from prestiging based on total resource generation
- **Meta-Upgrades** - Permanent improvements that persist across prestiges
  - *Persistent Speed* - Balls start faster each prestige
  - *Offline Progress* - Generate resources while away
  - *Head Start* - Begin each prestige with bonus resources

### Challenge System
- **Slow Motion Challenge** - Reduced ball speed but 3x resource multiplier
- **Minimalist Challenge** - Limited to 3 balls but 5x resource multiplier  
- **Inflation Challenge** - 10x costs but 20x passive income
- **Challenge Rewards** - Permanent efficiency improvements and unlock new content

### Milestones
- **Progress Tracking** - Achievement system based on reaching resource/action thresholds
- **Automatic Rewards** - Bonuses applied when milestones are completed
- **Progression Gates** - Some content unlocked through milestone completion

### Advanced Systems
#### Decay System
- **Vitality** - Ball health that decreases through collisions
- **Global Decay Rate** - Affects how quickly all balls lose vitality
- **Vitality Recovery** - Regeneration of ball health during collisions
- **Visual Decay Indicators** - Balls change color and show cracks as they decay
- **Decay Resistance** - Upgrades and buildings that slow or prevent decay

#### Automation
- **Auto-Click** - Automatically perform manual actions
- **Auto-Buy** - Purchase buildings automatically when affordable
- **Auto-Research** - Automatically unlock available research

#### Efficiency Systems
- **Ball Speed Efficiency** - Modifies how fast balls move
- **Production Efficiency** - Affects passive building output
- **Cost Efficiency** - Reduces upgrade and building costs

### UI Features
- **Progress Modal** - Detailed tracking of game progress and unlock conditions
- **Visual Effects** - Screen shake and collision effects for feedback
- **Responsive Design** - Mobile-compatible interface
- **Offline Progress** - Continue earning resources when not actively playing

### Progression Path
1. **Early Game** - Focus on basic upgrades, wall collisions, first buildings
2. **Mid Game** - Unlock intermediate buildings, research system, special balls
3. **Science Phase** - Ball-to-ball collisions, research unlocks, automation
4. **Prestige Preparation** - Reach 100K pixels or 50K energy for first prestige
5. **Meta Progression** - Prestige system, meta-upgrades, challenge system
6. **End Game** - Quantum/antimatter resources, decay nullification, max efficiency
